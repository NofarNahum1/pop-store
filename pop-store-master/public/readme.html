(1) Store Name: Pop-Up Store

(2) We sell Funko POP! figures - vinyl figurines of characters from pop culture, movies, and real life.

(3) The additional pages we added are:

  • pop-of-the-month.html:

    This page shows the Pop of the Month chosen by the admin along with a description.
    The admin can update the Pop of the Month and its description in the pop-of-the-month section of admin-dashboard.html, choosing only from Pops that are already in the store's inventory.


  • mystery-pop.html:

    This page gives clients an opportunity to enter a raffle for a mystery Pop.
    The user has three chances to guess correctly. when guessed correctly the user needs to fill his email and address

  • reviews.html and admin-reviews.html:

    This page allows clients to leave a review about their experience in our store.
    Simply write your review and click the 'Send' button on the reviews.html page.
    The admin can view all the submitted reviews on admin-reviews.html.

  • statistics.html:

    This page displays the store's sales statistics in a chart and a table for admins only.

    • Readme - Nir

    (1) Store Name: Pop-Up Store

    (2) We sell Pop-Up brand toys.

    (3) The additional pages we added are:

    • **Mystery Pop**: A page where users can guess the Pop and have a chance to receive a Pop-Up toy.
    • **statistics**: A page for the admin to view the best-selling Pop-Up toys.
    • **Feedback and Reviews**: A page where users can leave feedback and reviews. The admin can access these reviews.
    • **Pop of the Month**: As an admin, log in to the admin panel, select the product you wish to feature, and it will be prominently displayed as the Product of the Month.

    How to Operate the Additional Pages:

    • **Mystery Pop**: Navigate to the page, guess the Pop by typing the product name, and potentially win a Pop-Up toy.
    • **statistics**: Simply click on the page to view the POPs sales statistics.
    • **Feedback and Reviews**: Leave your feedback or review by typing in the provided input fields. If you are an admin, you need to log in to view and manage the feedback.
    • **Pop of the Month**: As an admin, log in to the admin panel, select the product you wish to feature and the users simpley will see it on the website

    (4) What was challenging:

    The most challenging part was integrating the front-end and back-end of the website to ensure smooth functionality across all pages.
